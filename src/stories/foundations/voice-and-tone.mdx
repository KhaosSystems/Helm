## Copywriting Guidelines

> I found this very difficult to write. Take this as a first draft. We should get some real professional copywriting help to refine this and make sure it's actually good.

Matter isn't a "visionary" product. <u>We are concise to save you time, friendly because we're on the same team, and honest because that's how you build better products.</u>

We don't use "marketing speak". We don't talk about "disrupting the industry" or "leveraging synergies". We are trying to build the next PostgreSQL, not MongoDB. We are reliable, stable, and built for professionals who spend 8+ hours a day in their tools. We are not trying to be flashy or trendy. We are trying to be the best tool for the job.

### Clear and Understandable (Anti-Jargon)

We use plain English. We are building complex tools, so our language should be simple. If a word sounds like a corporate buzzword, delete it.

- How to write it: Use strong, everyday verbs. Talk about the UI exactly as it appears.
- <span style={{ borderBottom: '2px solid #e53e3e' }}>
    Utilize our state-of-the-art shortcut architecture to optimize your workflow and achieve unparalleled efficiency.
  </span>
- <span style={{ borderBottom: '2px solid #38a169' }}>
    Global shortcuts are built-in. Define your keys once, and they work across the entire app.
  </span>

### Friendly, Honest and Responsible (Anti-Hype)

We don't overpromise. We're honest about our stack. We are on the same team as the user, which means we tell the truth. Trust is built by owning our constraints and working together to find solutions and improvements.

- **How to write it:** Write like you're talking to a peer on Slack. Admit when something isn't perfect, and be genuinely helpful.
- <span style={{ borderBottom: '2px solid #e53e3e' }}>
    Matter delivers a flawless, infinitely scalable experience for any conceivable application."
  </span>
- <span style={{ borderBottom: '2px solid #38a169' }}>
    Matter is optimized for high-density tooling. If you're building a marketing site, we are likely not the best fit.
  </span>

### Concise and Simple (The Anti-Fluff Rule)

Matter is build for professionals who spend 8+ hours a day in their tools. Every extra word is friction. We get to the point quickly, and only describe what is needed.

- **How to write it: **One idea per sentence. Front-load the action.
- <span style={{ borderBottom: '2px solid #e53e3e' }}>
    In order to facilitate a more streamlined selection process, users can now experience the convenience of hovering
    over options and pressing 1-9 on their keyboards.
  </span>
- <span style={{ borderBottom: '2px solid #38a169' }}>
    Hover and press 1-9 to select an option without opening the menu.
  </span>
