### Mobility and Ergonomics

For Matter, accessibility is more than a compliance, it's about efficiency and longevity. Our users live in these interfaces 8 hours a day. Every unnecessary mouse movement is a tax on their wrists. We treat keyboard-only navigation as a first-class feature; just ask the Vim crowd.

- **Minimize the Mouse:** We design for "zero-mouse" workflows. Between global shortcuts and the / command system, you can drive the entire UI from the home row.
- **Hover and Type:** We reduce clicks wherever possible. With features like MtSelect, you can simply hover and tap 1-9 to select an option. It's faster for power users and better for ergonomics.
- **Sane Hitboxes:** High-density doesn't mean "tiny." We keep hitboxes generous so that when you do reach for the mouse, you don't have to be a neurosurgeon to hit the target.
